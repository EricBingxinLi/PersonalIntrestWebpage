                                                           Insects

Insects are pancrustacean hexapod invertebrates and the largest group within the arthropod phylum.Usually, insects comprise a class within the Arthropoda.

Orchid Mantis, or Hymenopus coronatus, is a beautiful pink and white mantis with lobes on its legs that look like flower pentals. Although this species does not live on orchids, it does look remarkably well like a flower or orchid.

Cicadas are large insects that belong to the suborder Auchenorrhyncha along with other hemipterans, such as leafhoppers and spittlebugs. They are known for their loud, shrill noise and discarded shells (called exoskeletons) that cling to trees and other vegetation
